Carbon Credit Yield per SVO₂

Weighted average current price of carbon credits being distributed, divided by the price of SVO₂ (expressed as an annualised percentage yield).

Yc = (Vt × Fr) / Sc

Vt = transaction volume · Fr = platform fee rate · Sc = circulating supply. Distribution is recalculated automatically every 30 minutes.
