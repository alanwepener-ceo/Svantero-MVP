Why Lifecycle Management Matters

  • Prevents double-counting through a single, auditable chain of custody from verification to retirement.
  • Gives corporates and individuals full transparency over where their carbon credits came from and how they were used.
  • Supports ESG and regulatory disclosure with downloadable, on-chain-verifiable audit trails.
  • Ties SVO₂'s deflationary burn mechanism directly to real-world retirement activity.
