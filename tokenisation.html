Why Tokenisation Matters

Credibility

Every token is backed by an independently verified, registry-listed carbon credit — eliminating duplicate or unverifiable claims.

Liquidity

Tokenisation makes credits instantly tradeable on the Decentralised Carbon Exchange, unlocking liquidity that traditional registries cannot offer.

Traceability

On-chain records provide a permanent, auditable trail from original verification through to eventual retirement.

Tokenised Production-Linked Royalties

Alongside tokenised carbon credits, Svantero issues a second class of tokenised instrument: fractional, yield-bearing rights to a pro-rata share of verified production revenue from an underlying decarbonisation project. Each royalty right is structured as a regulated security, held by a bankruptcy-remote SPV, and issued as an ERC-3643 (T-REX) token — the same permissioned, compliance-gated standard that underpins the platform's Governance & Trust Layer below.

How Royalty Tokenisation Works

  • The underlying rights (land or harvesting rights, or credit-generation rights) are transferred into a bankruptcy-remote SPV, which is the legal issuer of record — not Svantero directly.
  • Verified production and sales data (tonnage, litres, or credits sold) is fed on-chain via oracle or multi-sig-verified reports.
  • Token holders receive their pro-rata royalty share automatically, distributed quarterly through a dedicated Revenue Distribution contract.
  • At maturity, the SPV repays the full principal per token, and tokens are burned on redemption.
  • Investor eligibility, transfer rules, and KYC/AML are enforced on-chain via ONCHAINID and the same Governance & Trust Layer framework used for tokenised carbon credits.

The Governance & Trust Layer

Sitting beneath the tokenisation engine is a governance and trust layer, conceptually modelled on institutional real-world-asset trust infrastructure: every tokenised carbon credit is bound to a defined trust tier, an independently signed verification event, and a live economic bond before it can be minted. Governance signals generated here are shared across the platform — they also appear on the Carbon Credit Lifecycle Management page, attached to the Verification and Audit stages, so every part of the ecosystem is working from the same source of truth.
